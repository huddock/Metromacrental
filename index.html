FAQ

What are the requirements?

Government ID, selfie with ID, and proof of billing or work/school ID. Refundable security deposit applies (amount depends on model and quantity).

Where do you deliver?

Anywhere within Metro Manila. Same‑day delivery available for bookings before 2 PM. Nearby Rizal/Cavite/Bulacan by arrangement.

What payment methods do you accept?

GCash, bank transfer (BPI/BDO/UnionBank), or cash on delivery for repeat customers.

What if the device is damaged or lost?

Minor wear is fine. For damage/loss, the renter covers repair or replacement, less normal depreciation. Optional damage protection available for a small fee.
